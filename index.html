Delicious Ramen Noodles

Description

Ramen noodles with a delicous chicken seasoning in a large bright bowl.

Ingredients

  • 30 oz Purified Water
  • 1 package of Ramen Noodles
  • 1 package of Ramen Noodles seasoning

Directions

  1. Heat pot till boiling.
  2. Open Ramen Noodle package and pour in its' contents into the boiling pot.
  3. Stir in up until the Noodles are boiled and soft.
  4. Add the packaged seasoning and stir until it is uniform with the other liquid.
  5. Pour hot pot's contents into a drainer.
  6. Move Ramen Noodles into a bowl.
  7. Wait until Noodles have cooled and ENJOY!
